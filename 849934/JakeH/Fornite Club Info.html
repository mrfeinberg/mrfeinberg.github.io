How to get into Fortnite Club

We meet twice a week and play Fortnite with Cool Kids Club.

You have to be in Cool Kids Club to be in Varsity Fortnite Club.

Every leader has to approve of you joining the Varsity Club.

The JV Club is open to anyone.
