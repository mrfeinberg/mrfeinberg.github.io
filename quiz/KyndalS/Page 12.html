The preppy aesthetic is different for girls and boys. Preppy girls wear oversized sweatshirts/t-shirts, hats, and tennis skirts. They wear bright colors like hot pink and green. The boys wear sweaters, vests, polos, button ups, and t-shirts. Preppy boys are known to wear expensive looking clothes and a very "neat" wardrobe.
