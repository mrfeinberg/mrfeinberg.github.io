Lacrosse Club

The Lacrosse Club is based off of the sport Lacrosse.
If you enjoy lacrosse or want to learn more about the game, I highly recommend that you join.

Lacrosse is one of the fastest growing games/sports in America. Lacrosse has been a game played for generations but has been slowly changing over the years. Lacrosse is a competitive team sport.The goal of the game is to get a little rubber ball into a goal/net. Each Player has a stick with a net on the end of it called a lacrosse stick. The size of the lacrosse sticks can vary, depending on what position you are. The players can run, carry, catch, shoot, and pass the ball with the net of the lacrosse stick. The lacrosse team with the most points or goals at the end of the time period wins the game. If you want to learn more about the rules of the game, click here>More Rules!
