About Us

Have you ever been listening to music and thought to yourself Nothing Was The Same? Well if so then I have the club for you! The Daddy Drake club is made for people who want to introduce new music into their lives and get More Life. If this sounds like you then feel free to join, you'll Thank me Later. Additionally if you are a Drake fan and would like to listen to some of his music then you are also free to join.



Why you should join The DDC

We here at the Daddy Drake Club take pride in our ability to provide a fun and engaging experience for every level of drake fan. It doesn't matter if you have never listened to drake before or if you are a lifelong fan, you will enjoy this club. If you're not convinced already, then this might change your mind. Every time we meet you will be provided with Pizza and soda.
