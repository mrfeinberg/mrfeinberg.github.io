FAQs

What do we do in Spider Meditation Club?

We explore our third eye along with these eight eyed creatures. We listen to relaxing audios while we do a variety of yoga poses and meditation methods.
The spiders will be only be released during meditation since there is a high chance of accidental death if they were to be released during yoga time.

What spiders are we using? Will I need protective gear?

Some spiders we will provide are the Goliath Birdeater , Huntsman Spider , Wolf Spider , Crab Spiders , and Orb Weaver spiders .
If you want to to bring in a spider or want a specific type, please email spidermeditationclubisthebestclubhahalosers@spiders.com.
As you can see, our spiders are cute and lovely. You will not need any protective gear!
