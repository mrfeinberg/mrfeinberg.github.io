About The Sport

Ping Pong is a sport like activity that was originated in England in the 19th century.
It is also called table tennis because it is on a small table and is similar to tennis.
It is played with red paddles and a little ball that is hollow and plastic.


Benefits of Joining

  • 1. We will bring some snacks and beverages
  • 2. We will have fun competitions
  • 3. Prizes for winning
