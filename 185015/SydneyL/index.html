*How to Become a Member*


If you are interested in joining the high class slip 'n slide club, please come talk to me as you will need to pass a series of tests.


*Below are the trials you will have to face*


-First you have to pass a written test on the different ratings of slides. If the majority of the club agrees with your statements, you pass. See the following link for reference

-Next you have to jump off of a standard high dive platform. Just for fun.

-And finally you have to make a slip 'n slide out of the limited materials that the club provides for you. These materials may include; duct tape, card board, a boot, wood chips, and a banana peel.


**current members of the club shall not be held accountable to preform the trials ever


*Requirements for Members*

All members will be required to have Wednesday and Saturday afternoons free as we will be traveling the country in search of the best slides.

The club members will also be required to slide down any slide I tell them to, in order to test for safety.
