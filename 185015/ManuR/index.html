About Our Club

The Cool Kids Club is an exclusive club for members with the upmost "swagg". We work in collaboration with the Fortnite Club and do most of our activities together so to be in this club you must also be in the Fortnite Club. THE CKC Club meets on Friday and only a select few are accepted into the club because it is the most prestigious club in the world. If you identify yourself as "cool" you should join this club.
