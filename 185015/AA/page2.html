Boba Instructions

Materials Needed for Bubble Tea

  • Tea (any type: black, green, jasmine, etc)

  • Milk or Sweetner

  • Tapioca Pearls (Boba)

  • Brown Sugar or Honey

  • Ice Cubes

  • Rice cooker/anything that can contain heat






Steps to Prepare Boba

Makes 6 Servings

  1. Gather materials

  2. Boil 6-8 cups of water at medium-high heat

  3. Put 1 cup boba into water

  4. Boil boba for 20-30 minutes

  5. Take boba out and let it sit in rice cooker for 20 minutes

  6. Repeat steps 4-5 again

  7. Put boba aside to make tea

Steps to Prepare the Drink

Makes 6 Serving

  1. Prepare your choice of tea

  2. Boil 1 cup of water and 1 cup of brown sugar on medium heat for 5-7 minutes

  3. Pour syrup into a cup(s), about 2cm up from the bottom of the cup

  4. Pour tea into cup, as much as you want

  5. If you want to, put cream/milk into your tea

  6. Enjoy!
